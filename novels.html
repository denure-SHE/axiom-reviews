About the Novels Programme

Axiom Review commissions and publishes serialized literary fiction that takes AI governance and organizational management seriously as subject matter — not as backdrop, but as the central drama. We believe fiction can explore the ethical, institutional, and human consequences of intelligent systems in ways that policy analysis alone cannot. Chapters are published as they are completed.
